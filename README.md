# Ocean Quest 🌊🐢

Help the Turtle and Axolotl save the ocean!

### 🎮 [Play the game live here!](https://nicksauer.github.io/ocean-game/)

**How to Play:**
- **Move:** Use Arrow Keys or WASD to swim.
- **Goal:** Collect coins and avoid the fish.
- **Level Up:**
  - 10 Coins: Level 2
  - 20 Coins: Level 3
  - 30 Coins: Level 4
  - 40 Coins: **FINAL BOSS**
- **Boss Fight:** Face the Giant Octopus! Press **Spacebar** or **Click** to shoot your collected coins at him to win. Watch out for his angry fish attacks!
